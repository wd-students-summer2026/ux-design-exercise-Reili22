I'd love to share a little more about my Golden Retriever. His name is Maomao, which means "fluffy" in Chinese, and it suits him perfectly. He's a 3-year-old boy with the most perpetually pouty expression. He never really looks happy, just quietly offended by everything, which makes him endlessly funny to be around.

He is incredibly clingy. He refuses to sleep in his own bed and instead parks himself right outside our bedroom door or beside the bed every single night. Honestly, I wouldn't have it any other way. I hope he stays by our side for a long, long time.
